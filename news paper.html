ICC Champions Trophy 2025

ICC Champions Trophy 2025 The ICC Champions Trophy 2025 was the ninth edition of the prestigious tournament, hosted by Pakistan Cricket Board. The tournament featured 15 matches held across three venues in Pakistan and one in the United Arab Emirates. India emerged as the champions after defeating New Zealand in the final, clinching a record-extending third title. The tournament marked the return of the ICC Champions Trophy after a hiatus and was the first major ICC tournament hosted by Pakistan in nearly three decades.
